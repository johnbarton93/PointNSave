Features

Feature List

Map

Map - The “Map” feature is a great way to draw attention to businesses or points of interest within your app. Users can easily access contact information, watch an about us video, and even access the social media of that business.

Events

Events - The "Events" feature is a great way for you to engage your users with easy event promotion. Add upcoming that allow users to add the event to their calendar and register for the event!

Resources

Resources - The "Resources" feature allows you to highlight the key resources in your community. This feature is categorized into three tiers. For example, Resource categories, Resource names, and Resource descriptions.

Directory

Directory - The "Directory" feature allows you to highlight the key businesses in your community. This feature is categorized into three tiers as well. For example, Business categories, Business names, and Business descriptions.
